Membership Information

Invest in the future of our business community and elevate Ray County together.

Welcome to the Richmond Area Chamber of Commerce

We are happy to help provide any answers to questions you may have regarding membership that you don’t get answered from the content below. Please contact us at 816-776-6916 or drop us a line here by using our application form below.

When you become a chamber member you invest in the future of the business community and ensure that the Chamber keeps working towards its mission. The Richmond Chamber works for every individual, business, and profession in the community.

Benefits of Joining Our Chamber

  • Listing in the Chamber Directory
  • Free Business Referrals
  • Ribbon Cutting Ceremonies
  • Business Before & After Hours and Luncheon Opportunities
  • Networking and Community Involvement
  • Promotional Materials and Business Card displayed in the Chamber’s office
  • Free Promotional posting on the Chamber’s social media platforms
  • Free use of Chamber Board Room and Resources
  • Business Appreciation Events
  • Access to Missouri State chamber Benefits

Additional Benefits for Elite Levels

Bronze

  • Highlighted in the member directory
  • Business name promoted on the monthly video spotlight

Silver

  • Highlighted in the member directory
  • Recognition on printed materials through Chamber
  • Video Spotlights along with monthly acknowledgements on spotlights
  • Member highlights on the Chamber website
  • Member mention at all Chamber events
